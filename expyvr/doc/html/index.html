Framework for Designing and Running Experiments in Virtual-Reality

This is the documentation page of the ExpyVR framework developed by the Laboratory of Cognitive Neuroscience at EPFL.

The ExpyVR framework is still in development. This guide might therefore not always be up-to-date.

Aknowledge the use of ExpyVR

Add this paragraph to the acknowledgement section of your paper;

This project was developed with ExpyVR, a multimedia and virtual reality platform for cognitive science 
experimentation developed at the Laboratory of Cognitive Neurosciences (http://lnco.epfl.ch) by Tobias Leugger, 
Nathan Evans and Bruno Herbelin.
